Meet Tom

Tom is a masculine name of Aramaic origin. A shortened version of Thomas, this name translates to “twin.” That being said, this name is still a wonderful choice if baby's joining your family as the one and only. What is a nickname for Tom? "Tom" or "Thom" are short versions of Thomas. "Tommy" is commonly used. The origin of the name Thomas can be traced back to the Aramaic name Ta'oma, which translates to "twin". The name gained popularity thanks to Saint Thomas, who was one of the twelve apostles of Jesus Christ.
